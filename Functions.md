# Functions

## Voice control

  Dario AI can respond to voice commands and speak to the user.

## Locale ai

  Dario AI uses local AI models via Ollama.

## Screen recognition

  Dario AI can analyze the screen and describe what is visible. This way he can also help you with troubleshooting or other tasks

## Hardware-diagnose

  Dario AI checks before every startup whether your hardware is compatible. If it is too weak, Dario AI will not start.
